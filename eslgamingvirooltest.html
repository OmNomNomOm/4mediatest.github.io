Logitech G continues as ESL's 2017 exclusive gear partner

Hans Oelschlägel
Editor / Content Manager
Share this article:
Tweet
132 total shares

Building on a successful tournament year in 2016, Logitech G and ESL will continue to work together for a multitude of competitions in 2017. Just like last year, all ESL One tournaments, the ESL CS:GO Pro League and the National Championships in France, Benelux, Germany and UK will enjoy top of the line support for all teams and players. Logitech G is set to provide them with the newest keyboards, headsets, mice and mousepads.

Ulrich Schulze, VP of Pro Gaming at ESL, is looking forward to the continued partnership.

“After an extraordinary year of competition in 2016, we here at ESL are excited to extend our partnership with Logitech G for another year of gripping ESL One, ESL Pro League and National Championship events. Their tournament-grade gear will allow the players to focus solely on their matches and bring out the best of their abilities on the big stage to deliver those legendary esports moments that we love to see.”

Logitech were already directly involved in ESL One Genting, 2017’s first esports tournament which concluded last weekend. And together with Logitech we can’t wait to find out what else 2017 will have in store for us across all competitions.

"Working with ESL, the world's largest esports company, is extremely important to us,"  said Ujesh Desai, general manager and vice president of Logitech G. "As the exclusive hardware provider we're excited to provide everyone at ESLaccess to all of our latest and greatest Pro gaming gear."

You can familiarize yourself with Logitech’s products by checking out their website and following their Twitter and Facebook accounts.
