Vega Squadron's new roster succeeds in trial by fire

posted by Abelle, 1 hour ago
Vega Squadron recently announced that they're trying out some new players in official matches. With a win in their opening Starladder | i-League Invitational #4 qualifier match, these two fresh faces might be exactly what Vega needs.

Since this roster was only announced for the match against HunkysFromZavod, it's not clear whether the team plans to keep these players in the long run. The two new players are Nikita 'Palantimos' Grinkevich and Johny 'Blizzy' Ree, two relative newcomers to the CIS scene. With the new players, Ilya 'AlohaDance' Korobkin has returned to the support role to make room for Palantimos. Blizzy is playing offlane to fill in for Vasily 'Afterlife' Shishkin, who was benched earlier this month.

Their teamwork was put to the test last night in a do-or-die situation. The StarLadder qualifiers are single elimination, and Vega very nearly didn't make it past the first round. Their opponents, HFZ, made a comeback in the first game and held a strong lead late into the second game, but Vega managed to make a comeback of their own before sealing the deal with a dominant performance in Game 3. According to Vega's coach, some motivational speeches may have played a part.
