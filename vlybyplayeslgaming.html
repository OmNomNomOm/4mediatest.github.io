About ESL Play

ESL Play is part of the ESL Gaming network, the world's largest, independent esports brand, leading the industry across various games with numerous online and offline competitions. Take the first step towards your esports career right now by registering and participating for free!

  • Over 1,000 grassroots leagues per month to improve your skill
  • Over 50,000 € prize money per month in our Major tournaments
  • Over 20 sponsors making it possible to provide you ESL Play for free
  • Over 1,000 volunteers from over 60 countries taking care of your needs
  • Over 7,000,000 users have been already registered

Register now!

Our services

ESL Play is the place to start your esports career. Start in grassroots competitions and continuously improve your skills to make your way from Amateur to Pro. We accompany you on your way and support you with several services.

  • Competitions for different skill levels + over 50 countries
  • Support and handling of match protests in all games
  • A free Anti Cheat system in several games
  • Prizes and prize money in almost every game
  • Awards, level points and rankings to record your success

Our games

ESL Play offers you a great variety of games in multiple genres for more than 15 years now, not only focusing on main titles but also following your, the community's, wishes! Your favorite game is esports friendly, offers custom lobbies but is missing on ESL? Let us know in the ESL forum and we may add it!

  • More than 60 games per month
  • Multiple genres like sports, racing, beat em up or shooter
  • Multiple platforms (PC, PlayStation, Xbox and Mobile)
Check out our New, Classic or Fun Game sections, if you can't find your game immediately.

Our league systems

To make playing on ESL for you as exciting and varied as possible, we have different types of competitions to fit your needs. Dividing the skill levels by Open/Major/Pro guarantees that you compete against players on a similar level and leads you step by step from Amateur to Pro status.

  • Cups (ko-system; one- or more-day tournaments)
  • Ladder (ranking system; schedule your matches)
  • League (group stage with fix matchdates)

How to participate

You want to participate in an ESL competition? It's pretty easy. Just follow these steps!

  1. Register for free!
  2. Choose a game
  3. Choose a competition
  4. Use the sign-up widget on the right side of our page
    1. Cups/Leagues: Wait for an opponent
    2. Ladders: Challenge other players or teams by using the matchmaking tools like the Instant Challenger or Matchmaker

How to play a match

As soon as you have an opponent it's all about the match! You'll be able to compete against players from all around the world, make new friends or perhaps even create rivalries. But how does the match process work?

  1. Use the gameaccount to add your opponent to contact him in-game
  2. Create a game lobby and invite your opponent
  3. Set up the correct settings / rules in-game
  4. Play your match
  5. Enter or confirm the result on ESL Play
  6. Did you experience any problems? Open a protest.

Our support

Our support team consists of experienced admins that want to make some contribution to their favorite game and its community. To offer you the best support, it is necessary that you explain your problem or question as detailed as possible. For international competitions it is mandatory to write your ticket in English, otherwise the admins might not understand your request.

  • Submit a ticket
  • Expect an answer within the first 72 hours
  • Please check our F.A.Q. for general questions
