FACEIT have opened sign-ups for the Boston Major Open Qualis

posted by Orange Juice, 2 hours ago
The Boston Major 2016 is just around the corner. Gather your crew and register for the open qualifiers, you never know you might get lucky. More details inside.






Before the year ends, Valve will once again light the fire in the Dota scene as the best teams in the world compete for another title. A title that will prove which teams have found their perfect matches of skills and chemistry after the chaotic roster shuffle - The Boston Major 2016.

FACEIT will organize the open qualifiers for Europe, SEA, and Americas; while Perfect World will organize for China. As has become customary, FACEIT also offers a chance for solo players to join forces 30 minutes before the start of the qualifiers if there are spaces left.

Rounds played: Round of 1024 (BO1) to Round of 32 (BO1)

SEA?—?Start time: 17:00 SGT
Europe?—?Start time: 17:00 CEST
Americas?—?Start time: 16:00 CDT

Rounds played: Round of 16 (BO1) to Finals (BO3).

FACEIT stated that the team has been testing new tools which they will test out in a "test tournament" on the 22nd October, a day before the 1st Open Qualifier. If you're looking for a warm-up or interested in some free loot, you can find more information on that tournament can be found here. You can also join their Discord channel.
